Our Legacy

Established in 2015, Clyde Hire has redefined luxury transportation in Kenya. Beginning with an exclusive collection of 8 premium vehicles, we've evolved into the nation's premier destination for sophisticated automotive experiences.

Our philosophy centers on delivering unparalleled elegance, supreme comfort, and impeccable service. Whether you're attending corporate events, celebrating special occasions, or exploring scenic destinations, we ensure your journey reflects your distinction.
